We are happy to announce the 2026 workshops and events that will be organized by members and with support of Instruct.SI. Here is a tentative list, the exact titles may still change:
- Workshop: ***De Novo* Protein Design for Beginners** (Linking Artificial Intelligence and Experimental Biochemistry), tentatively in September 2026 (*University of Ljubljana, Biotechnical Faculty*)
- Workshop: **Introduction to Phage Display Technology and Computational Methods for the Isolation of Binding Proteins**, 23–25 September 2026 (*University of Nova Gorica*)
- Workshop: **Cryo-EM Workshop, Single Particle Analysis**, 10–12 November 2026 (*National Institute of Chemistry*)
- Workshop: **Biophysical Characterization of Proteins**, 2–3 December 2026 (University of Ljubljana, Faculty of Chemistry and Chemical Technology)
- Symposium: **One-day scientific symposium**, at *National Institute of Chemistry*, 18 November 2026 (Instruct.SI members)

Please follow our announcements on this website and other channels (e.g., mailing list of Slovenian Biochemical Society) for additional information.
